About Us!

Hello! I’m Liang Ling, a dog-mom who believes that learning should always be fun — for both humans and dogs. This site is inspired by my dog Miso, a curious little Pomapoo who turns every training session into a comedy show. When I started teaching Miso simple tricks, I realised that most online guides felt too serious or text-heavy. So I introduce you to Basic Pawgress! A site so you can have fun with your furry friend, in the most light-hearted way.
