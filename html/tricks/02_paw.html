Paw

1   Sit

Start your dog in a "sit" position for the foundation of the trick.

2   Entice

Hold a high-value treat in your closed fist. Your dog will likely try to paw at your hand to get the treat.

3   Mark and Reward

When they lift their paw, gently shake and reward!
